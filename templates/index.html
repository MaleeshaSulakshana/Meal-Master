About Us

The Meal Master website is a all in one application for recipe recommendation and meal planning needs. Through this platform users are about to share, retrieve and browse recipe and leave remakes on the current recipe within the Meal Master Community

Through the Meal Master website our member can get access to meal plan for 3 meal for 7 days by simply answering our questions.

What our website is trying to implement is healthy eating habit to the community as people nowadays have become so accustomed to quick instant food that has a very negative impact on human heath in both short and long run. Therefor Meal Master main objective is transform healthy eating behavior.
